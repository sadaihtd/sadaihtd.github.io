About Sad Dev Studio

Passion for pixels, code, and storytelling.

Sad Dev Studio is a one-person independent game studio dedicated to crafting meaningful mobile experiences. Founded in 2026, the mission is simple: create games that respect your time and intelligence.

Every project is a labor of love, built from scratch with a focus on clean design, smooth gameplay, and no predatory monetization. When you play a Sad Dev Studio game, you're playing something made by a gamer, for gamers.
